About

People who walk for Water believe that Water has spirit, that Water is alive and Water sustains all life. Humans are 60% water and the earth is 70% water. Yet water is becoming a commodity instead of a basic human right. And the earth is treated as an endless resource for abuse.

This mobile Water Protector community is retracing the steps of Anishnaabe who migrated west in search of Manoomin - food that grows on the water - aka wild rice. The prophecies foretell a reverse migration to recover what was lost along the way.

We walk in prayer for unity and healing of spirits!
